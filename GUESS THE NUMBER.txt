GUESS THE NUMBER

1. The application generates a random number from 1 to 20.
2. You write a number from 1 to 20 in the box and click the check box.
3. You get a hint for every try if the number is too high or too low.
4. For every try your score decreases by 1.
5. When you guess the right number your highscore becomes your current score.
6. You can press the "Again!" button to play again without losing your highscore.
